Rules & Guidelines

Community standards for quality roleplay

General Rules

  • Respect all community members at all times
  • No harassment, discrimination, or toxic behavior
  • Follow Discord Terms of Service and Community Guidelines
  • Use appropriate language in all communications
  • No spam or excessive self-promotion

Roleplay Rules

  • Stay in character during roleplay scenarios
  • No random death matching (RDM) or vehicle death matching (VDM)
  • Realistic roleplay is required at all times
  • Follow proper radio protocols and procedures
  • No metagaming or powergaming
  • Respect other players' roleplay scenarios

Department Specific Rules

  • Follow your department's standard operating procedures
  • Maintain professional conduct while on duty
  • Use appropriate equipment and vehicles for your rank
  • Attend mandatory training sessions
  • Report incidents through proper channels

Consequences

  • Warning: First offense for minor violations
  • Kick: Repeated minor violations or moderate offenses
  • Ban: Serious violations or continued rule breaking
  • Important: Rejoining within 30 minutes of a kick results in an automatic ban
  • Appeal Process: Available through our report system
