About Royal Military Police

The Royal Military Police (RMP) in UKBRUM is a specialized military police force responsible for maintaining discipline, investigating military crimes, and providing policing services within the armed forces community. Our officers are tasked with upholding military law and supporting operational security.

As a member of the RMP, you'll participate in military investigations, provost duties, and specialized military policing operations. We emphasize military discipline, professional conduct, and adherence to military protocols.

Key Responsibilities

  • Investigate military crimes and disciplinary matters
  • Provide close protection for military personnel
  • Conduct provost duties and military policing
  • Support military operations with security services
  • Maintain discipline within military establishments
