Specialized Units

UKBRUM features a variety of specialized units that members can join based on their interests and skills. These units operate independently and may work with any of the main departments as needed.

Firearms Unit

Specially trained officers who respond to incidents involving firearms or threats requiring armed support.

  • Armed response vehicles
  • Counter-terrorism operations
  • High-risk warrant execution

Traffic Unit

Specialized officers who enforce traffic laws and investigate serious road traffic collisions.

  • High-speed pursuit capability
  • Advanced driver training
  • Collision investigation

Dog Section

Officers who work with specially trained police dogs to track suspects, find missing persons, and detect contraband.

  • Tracking and apprehension
  • Drug and explosive detection
  • Public order support

Special Escort Group

Elite officers who provide armed escorts for high-value assets and VIPs.

  • Advanced driving techniques
  • Close protection training
  • Counter-ambush tactics

Marine Unit

Specialized officers who provide security for naval bases and waterways.

  • Maritime patrol vessels
  • Underwater search capability
  • Port security operations

Tactical Support Group

Highly trained officers who provide tactical support for high-risk operations.

  • Advanced weapons training
  • Tactical entry techniques
  • Specialized equipment

Technical Rescue

Specialized team trained in complex rescue operations including building collapse and confined spaces.

  • Urban search and rescue
  • Rope rescue operations
  • Structural collapse response

Hazardous Materials

Specially trained team who respond to incidents involving hazardous materials and substances.

  • Chemical identification
  • Decontamination procedures
  • Specialized protective equipment

Water Rescue

Team trained in water rescue operations for floods and water-related emergencies.

  • Swift water rescue
  • Flood response
  • Boat operations

HART Team

Hazardous Area Response Team - trained to provide medical care in dangerous environments.

  • Chemical incident response
  • Working at heights
  • Confined space operations

Air Ambulance

Specialized team that provides rapid response and transport via helicopter for critical patients.

  • Rapid deployment
  • Advanced medical equipment
  • Critical care capability

Critical Care

Advanced team with additional training to provide enhanced medical interventions.

  • Advanced clinical procedures
  • Specialized medications
  • Trauma management

Incident Support

Specialized team who provide enhanced support during major road incidents.

  • Advanced traffic management
  • Scene preservation
  • Multi-agency coordination

Vehicle Recovery

Specialized team equipped to recover and remove disabled or damaged vehicles from highways.

  • Heavy vehicle recovery
  • Rapid clearance techniques
  • Specialized equipment

Winter Operations

Team responsible for keeping roads clear and safe during adverse winter weather.

  • Snow plowing
  • Salt spreading
  • Weather monitoring
