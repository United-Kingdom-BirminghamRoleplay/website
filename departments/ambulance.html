About West Midlands Ambulance Service

The West Midlands Ambulance Service (WMAS) in UKBRUM is dedicated to providing high-quality pre-hospital care to those in need. Our paramedics and emergency medical technicians respond to a wide range of medical emergencies, from minor injuries to life-threatening conditions.

As a member of WMAS, you'll participate in realistic medical emergency scenarios, training exercises, and patient care simulations. We emphasize medical knowledge, quick decision-making, and compassionate patient care in all aspects of our service.

Key Responsibilities

  • Respond to emergency medical calls
  • Provide pre-hospital emergency care
  • Transport patients to appropriate medical facilities
  • Coordinate with other emergency services during major incidents
  • Maintain medical equipment and supplies
