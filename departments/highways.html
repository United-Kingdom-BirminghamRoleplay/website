National Highways

Ensuring safe and efficient travel on Britain's major roads

About National Highways

National Highways in UKBRUM is responsible for operating, maintaining, and improving England's motorways and major A roads. Our traffic officers work to keep traffic flowing smoothly, assist broken-down vehicles, and manage incidents to minimize disruption.

As a member of National Highways, you'll participate in realistic traffic management scenarios, incident response, and roadside assistance operations. We emphasize safety, efficiency, and customer service in all aspects of our work.

Key Responsibilities

  • Patrol motorways and major roads
  • Respond to incidents and breakdowns
  • Manage traffic flow during incidents
  • Clear debris and hazards from roadways
  • Coordinate with emergency services during major incidents
