Community Disclaimer

United Kingdom Birmingham Roleplay ("UKBRUM") is an independent roleplay community for Emergency Response: Liberty County on Roblox. We are not affiliated with Roblox Corporation, Emergency Response: Liberty County developers, or any official entities. All game content, logos, and assets belong to their respective owners. UKBRUM is a fan-operated community server.

Terms of Participation

By joining our community, you agree to:

  • Follow all Roblox Terms of Service and Community Standards
  • Comply with Discord Terms of Service when using our Discord server
  • Adhere to all community rules and staff directives
  • Maintain respectful behavior toward all community members
  • Be at least 13 years old (Roblox's minimum age requirement)

We reserve the right to suspend, restrict, or permanently ban any member who violates these terms or disrupts the community environment. Disciplinary actions may be taken without prior warning at staff discretion.

Code of Conduct

All community members must:

  • Respect others: No harassment, discrimination, or bullying based on race, gender, religion, sexuality, or any other characteristic
  • Protect privacy: Never share personal information (yours or others') including real names, addresses, phone numbers, or social media accounts
  • Play fairly: No exploiting, cheating, or using unauthorized modifications
  • Communicate appropriately: Keep language clean and conversations appropriate for all ages
  • Follow roleplay rules: Stay in character during sessions and respect established scenarios

Community Rules & Enforcement

All members must follow our community rules covering roleplay conduct, Discord behavior, and department-specific guidelines. Rules include:

  • Roleplay Standards: Realistic scenarios, proper character development, and appropriate interactions
  • Communication: Respectful language in Discord channels and voice chat
  • Department Rules: Specific protocols for police, fire, medical, and civilian roles
  • Application Requirements: Age limits, activity expectations, and interview processes

Enforcement: Rule violations result in warnings, temporary bans, or permanent removal based on severity. Staff decisions are final but can be appealed through proper channels.

Privacy & Data Protection

Website: This website uses basic functionality to display community information. We do not collect personal data or use tracking cookies. The site includes accessibility features that store preferences locally on your device.

Discord Server: Our Discord server uses bots for community management:

  • Melonly Bot: Handles applications, member verification, and server automation
  • Moderation Tools: Log messages and user activity for rule enforcement
  • Support System: Creates transcripts of support tickets reviewed by Senior High Ranking staff for quality assurance

We collect Discord usernames, IDs, and message content only for server moderation and community management. No personal information is shared with third parties. Data is retained only as long as necessary for community operations.

Intellectual Property

Original content created by UKBRUM (logos, documentation, custom assets) remains our intellectual property. Community members retain rights to their own original creations. We respect all third-party intellectual property and use external assets under appropriate licenses with proper attribution.

Limitation of Liability

UKBRUM, its staff, and affiliates are not liable for:

  • Any damages or losses resulting from community participation
  • Actions of other community members
  • Technical issues with Roblox, Discord, or third-party services
  • Loss of virtual items, progress, or accounts

Participation is entirely at your own risk. We recommend following online safety guidelines and parental supervision for minors.

Enforcement & Appeals

Staff decisions regarding rule violations are made fairly but are final. Members may appeal disciplinary actions through our official appeal process. Repeated violations may result in permanent removal from the community.

Contact & Support

For questions, concerns, or to report issues:

  • Discord Support: Use ticket system or contact staff directly
